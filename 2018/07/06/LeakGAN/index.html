LeakGAN

Introduction

LeakGAN其实上就是SeqGAN的一个加强版，不同于SeqGAN仅利用Discriminator反馈的reward来训练Generator，LeakGAN还通过通过引入Discriminator隐层提取出的高级特征（leaked information）来指导Generator进行序列生成，使得模型的长序列生成性能得到显著的提高。此外LeakGAN的实验中还利用了若干trick解决了在训练SeqGAN中遇到的一些问题。

从上面的例子可以看出其生成长文本的能力很差，主要原因在于：

  1. Reward 包含的信息量对于长文本生成来说太少
  2. Generator所采用的LSTM网咯并不具备长期记忆能力

为了解决第一个问题LeakGAN模型把Discriminator的隐层（卷积池化层）输出的feature map作为leaked information来训练Manager生成goal vector，Worker再利用goal vector包含的高阶信息（一般是语句结构上的信息）和Discriminator最后反馈的reward来进行序列生成，以此来解决reward信息量不够的问题。

Model structure

Discriminator-Feature Extractor CNN

$f_t=\digamma(S_t; \phi_f)$

Generator-Manager LSTM

$\hat{gt}, h^m_t=M(f_t, h^m{t-1}; \theta_m)$

$g_t=\hat{g_t}/||\hat{g_t}||$

$wt=W{\psi}(\sum^c{i=1}g{t-i})$

Generator-Worker LSTM

$Ot, h_t^{w}=W(x_t, h^w{t-1}; \theta_w)$

$G{\theta}(x{t+1}|s_t)=softmax(O_t\centerdot w_t/\alpha)$

Model training

Generator training

Manager和Worker交替训练，训练其中一个的时候要固定另外一个的参数（当然Discriminator的参数这时也是固定的）。Manager被训练用来估计高层次的序列生成方向（advantageous directions），Worker被训练用来跟随Manager输出的序列生成方向（follow the directions）。

Gradient of Manager $\nabla_{\theta_m}g_t$

  • Expected reward under current policy

    $E[r_t]=Q(f_t, g_t)=Q_F(s_t, g_t)$

  • Cosine similarity between change of feature representation and goal vector

    $d{cos}(f{t+c}-f_t, g_t(\theta_m))$

  • Policy gradient

    $\nabla{\theta_m}g_t=-Q_F(s_t, g_t)\nabla{\thetam}d{cos}(f_{t+c}-f_t, g_t(\theta_m))$

Gradient of Worker

  • Intrinsic reward

    $r^It=\frac{1}{c}\sum^c{i=1}d{cos}(f_t-f{t-i}, g_{t-i})$

  • Expected intrinsic reward over all possible actions

    $\sum{x_t}r^I_tW(x_t|s{t-1}; \theta_w)$

  • Expected intrinsic reward over all possible actions & all current states

    $E{s{t-1}\sim G}[\sum{x_t}r^I_tW(x_t|s{t-1}; \theta_w)]$

  • Policy gradient

    $\nabla{\theta_w}E{s{t-1}\sim G}[\sum{xt}r^I_tW(x_t|s{t-1}; \theta_w)]$

    $=E{s{t-1}\sim G}[\sum{x_t}r^I_t\nabla{\theta_w}W]$

    $=E{s{t-1}\sim G}[\sum{x_t}r^I_tW\frac{\nabla{\theta_w}W}{W}]$

    $=E{s{t-1}\sim G, xt\sim W}[r^I_t\nabla{\theta_w}logW]$

Problem 2 — Gradient Vanishing

There will be a severe gradient vanishing problem during the training of GAN when D is much stronger than G. The reward is too small to update the parameters in G. Therefore it needs to be rescaled.

Trick — Bootstrapped Rescaled Activation

Reward matrix is $R_{B*T}$ with batch-size B and sequence-length T.

Rescale it by doing $R_{i,t} = \sigma(\delta\centerdot(0.5-\frac{rank(i)}{B}))$.

  • Expectation and variance of the reward in each mini-batch are constant!
  • In general, all ranking model can prevent the gradient vanishing problem (More explanation — RankGAN).
